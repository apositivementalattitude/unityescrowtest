Privacy Policy

Unity Escrow Inc. will never ask you for any type of personal information or to wire funds from your bank by email alone without face to face meeting and/or reliable verification.

  1. Home
  2. Privacy Policy

Unity Escrow Inc. values your privacy.

If you receive an email that appears to be from Unity Escrow Inc. and the message asks you to wire funds, move your money and/or for personal information such as your social security number, bank account or transaction number, or other sensitive information, DO NOT RESPOND or click any links in the email; CONTACT our office or your Escrow Officer immediately TO CONFIRM all communication from us to you.

Beware of “phishing" it is an attempt by an internet fraudster to trick you into sharing your personal information. This type of email fraud targets customers of legitimate companies and is being used with great frequency by internet criminals. Typically, the email warns you of some problem with an account or promotes a special offer and directs you to a website that appears to belong to a legitimate company. In fact, the website is spoofed and any information you provide may be used fraudulently and you could become a victim of Identity Theft.

Privacy Safety Tips:

  • Keep your software (Operating system and internet browser) up-to-date.
  • LOOK AT the email address for misspellings or slightly altered spelling to fool you. Confirm the email address before receiving or sending emails.
  • Make sure you have anti-virus software and a personal firewall enabled and current on your home PC.
  • Be suspicious of any email that asks for personal information, requests your authentication, or indicates a problem with your Unity Escrow Inc. account.
  • Be aware that legitimate companies do not ask customers to verify personal information by sending email or pop-up ads.
  • Never respond to an email asking for your credit card information.
  • Confirm and Verify with our team before sending personal and financial information by email. – Verify with our office.
  • Never click on a link in an email if you are unsure of its origins, especially if the email asks for personal financial information.
  • If you receive a questionable email that contains Unity Escrow Inc. name or logo, do not reply by email and do not click on any links.
